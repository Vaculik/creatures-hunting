Creatures hunting - information system

About the information system:

After a nuclear disaster there are a lot of mutants, zombies and other creatures wandering through some areas of our world. A handful of survivals must defend themselves agains these creatures. There are available various kinds of weapons, each weapon has different useability against some monster. This system should enable storing information about monsters (name, height, weight, agility, ...), areas where the monsters live (name, description, area) and available weapons (name, gun-reach, ammunition used). Aministrator of the system should be able to perform CRUD operations on all entities. Ordinary user will use this system to record his experience which weapon could be efficiently used against the monster as well as to assign areas where the creature has been spotted.

About the web:

The web of the system is divided into 5 parts.

Home

  • basic information about the system.


Creatures

  • Users may display list of all creatures, the highest and the heaviest creatures.

  • More information about the creature is available after click on the chosen creature.

  • Users see creature name, type, height, eight and description.

  • Users see the most efficient weapons against the chosen creature and all creatures of the same type (may click on others creatures)

  • Click here

Weapons

  • Users may display list of all weapons.

  • More information about the weapon is available after click on the chosen weapon.

  • Users see weapon name, type, description, ammo type and range.

  • Users see the creatures which are the weapons the most efficient to, all weapon with the same type and with the same ammo type.

  • Click here


Area

  • Users may display list of all areas, the areas with no or any creature and the areas where are creatures the most and the fewest.

  • More information about the area is available after click on the chosen area.

  • Users see area name, description and list of creatures which are in the area.

  • Click here


Users

  • Users may display list of all users, men, women, ordinary users and admins.

  • More information about the user is available after click on the chosen user.

  • Users see user name, username, type, sex and date of birth.

  • Click here
